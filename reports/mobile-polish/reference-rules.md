## Allowed To Borrow

- Clear four-destination bottom navigation with an obvious active state.
- Mobile-first single-column content flow and roomy tap targets.
- A compact home screen that puts primary destinations near the top.
- Repeated card patterns for feeds, services, settings groups, and profile summaries.
- Predictable stacked list/detail layouts for notifications, FAQ/help, and contacts.
- Compact grouped settings rather than long uninterrupted forms.

## Forbidden To Copy

- Exact screen order, Hebrew labels, or feature naming from the reference.
- Exact icon family, icon-in-circle styling, or button outlines.
- The reference palette, especially pale pink/white balance.
- Header imagery style, photo treatment, or decorative motifs.
- Exact card geometry, grid arrangement, or spacing ratios.

## AMS Translation Rules

- Keep AMS on the existing warm premium palette and display typography.
- Prefer richer contrast, deeper surfaces, and calmer hierarchy than the reference.
- Use one dominant accent per screen and reduce competing surface treatments.
- Motion should clarify focus and affordance, not create spectacle.
- Mobile layouts must continue to work in RTL, dark theme, and PWA standalone mode.
